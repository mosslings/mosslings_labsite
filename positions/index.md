## Ph.D. Positions Available

Highly motivated M.Sc. and B.Tech graduates with a strong interest in plant developmental biology, and physiology are encouraged to apply to the lab through the official Ph.D. admission calls of IISER-TVM.

Students interested in exploring questions in moss biology, carbon sequestration, plant development, environmental sensing, and image-based phenotyping are especially encouraged to apply.

## Minor Project Positions Available

Curious and motivated IISER-TVM students who enjoy tinkering with Raspberry Pi, environmental sensors, or coding are encouraged to apply for semester-long minor projects through the PI’s email.

Projects may involve building low-cost scientific instruments like environmental monitoring systems and image analysis pipeline workflows.
